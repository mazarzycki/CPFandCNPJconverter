How to use this tool

In order to add special characters to CPF or CNPJ just insert the number (by pasting or writing) into the correct field. It will be automatically transformed and you can copy it.
If you need to remove special characters insert your number into the correct field and click the button. As a result you will get the digits sequence without special characters.
